About AVTNC

Cochlea.nz is provided by AVTNC. Auditory and Vestibular Translational research cluster is a research group at the University of Auckland. We are passionate about understanding how human hearing and balance systems work. According to the World Report on Hearing published by the World Health Organization, 1.5 billion people are affected by hearing loss globally. This number is expected to increase to 2.5 billion by 2050, and novel strategies for preventing and treating hearing loss is needed. Our cluster carry out research projects aimed to improve therapeutic outcomes for people with hearing and balance disorders for New Zealanders.

The Team

We are based at the Grafton campus of the University of Auckland, located opposite to the Auckland hospital. Current members consist of a mixture of researchers and post-graduate students at the University of Auckland. We also work with collaborators at the University of Otago and other research institutions in New Zealand, as well as from overseas.
